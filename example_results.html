Using ABBA to find differential methylation between WKY and LWS rats.

As part of our recent paper we used ABBA to analyse WGBS data from two rat strains. This was both a demonstration of the usefulness of ABBA but also revealed some novel biological processes that may underpin glomerulonephritis. Below is the output from running ABBA on the rat WGBS data as an example of what to expect from using ABBA on your own data.
